# 🏥 MedVocab-Pro: Advanced Medical & Pharmaceutical Glossary

Welcome to **MedVocab-Pro**, a specialized digital repository designed to bridge the gap between clinical pharmacology, academic English (C1/C2), and linguistic etymology.

## 🎯 Project Goals
* **Precision:** To provide evidence-based clinical definitions.
* **Academic Excellence:** To master high-level English vocabulary for IELTS (7.5+) and professional practice.
* **Etymological Depth:** To explore the Greek and Latin roots of medical terminology.
* **AI Integration:** Leveraging advanced AI workflows for systematic data structuring.

## 🛠 Methodology & Standards
Every entry in this glossary follows a strict **Multidimensional Analysis** format:
1. **Etymology:** Linguistic roots.
2. **Clinical Definition:** Based on MSD Manuals/FDA guidelines.
3. **Pharmacological Context:** MOA and therapeutic relevance.
4. **Academic Usage:** Contextual sentence for C2 English mastery.
5. **Sources:** Peer-reviewed references.
